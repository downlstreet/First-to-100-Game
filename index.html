First to 500

    Rules
  1. Roll your three dice.
  2. The values of your dice add to your score.
  3. Extra points are awarded for pairs and triplets (see scoring below).
  4. The first player to 1000 points wins!

    Points
  • Each dice it's worth it's own number value.
  • A Pair of One's = 10 points
  • A Pair of Two's = 20 points
  • A Pair of Three's = 30 points
  • A Pair of Four's = 40 points
  • A Pair of Five's = 50 points
  • A Pair of Six's = 60 points
  • Triplets = 100 points
